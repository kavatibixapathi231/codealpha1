# IoT and Artificial Intelligence Integration (AIoT)

## 📖 Overview

This mini project explores the integration of the Internet of Things (IoT) and Artificial Intelligence (AI), commonly known as AIoT (Artificial Intelligence of Things). The project examines how IoT devices collect real-time data and how AI analyzes that data to enable intelligent decision-making, automation, and predictive analytics across various industries.

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Understand the fundamentals of IoT and AI.
- Explore the architecture of AI-integrated IoT systems.
- Study real-world applications of AIoT.
- Analyze the benefits and challenges of AIoT implementation.
- Examine future trends and developments in AIoT technology.
